# SQC short-form hooks/scripts — TikTok, Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts

Purpose: funny/meme-like short content that makes Side Quest Chess feel instantly tryable and shareable.

Format target: 8-25 seconds each, vertical 9:16.

Reusable CTA options:

- `Try the quest at sidequestchess.com`
- `Send this to the chess friend who deserves suffering`
- `Start with No Castle Club if you are emotionally stable`
- `Earn proof when the bad idea works`

## Production template

1. Open with a chess pain point or absurd command.
2. Show the SQC quest card/rule.
3. Show a tiny gameplay/result/proof beat.
4. End with a dare.

## 10 hooks + scripts

### 1. The queen is overrated. Probably.

**Hook text:** `Lose your queen before move 15. Still win.`

**Script:**

- 0:00: Board clip: queen gets captured. Sound: record scratch.
- 0:02: Cut to SQC card: `Queen? Never Heard of Her`.
- 0:04: VO/text: “Normal chess: resign. Side Quest Chess: badge opportunity.”
- 0:08: Flash proof/badge: `Certified Queenless Maniac`.
- 0:11: CTA: “Try it if you have no survival instinct.”

**Caption:** The queen is mostly decorative, right?

## Platform notes

### TikTok

- Lead with the joke, not the product name.
- Use comments as prompt: “Which quest is most cursed?”
- Pin a comment with the direct quest link only after the post gets initial traction.

### Instagram Reels

- Prioritize visual proof: quest cards, coats of arms, proof scrolls.
- Captions should be short and meme-like.
- Stories can reshare proof and polls: `Which side quest should we suffer through next?`

### YouTube Shorts

- Strongest fit for tutorial cutdowns and chess challenge phrasing.
- Reuse the long YouTube demo footage.
- End cards/comments should route to the full demo and SQC homepage.
